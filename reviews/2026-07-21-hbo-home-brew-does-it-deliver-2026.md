Summary This starter kit covers the core hardware—a food-grade jar, a useful thermometer, and a SCOBY with starter liquid—for brewing one gallon of kombucha at home. Who This Is For This kit suits someone who has read about kombucha brewing and wants a straightforward hardware bundle without paying for extras they don't need. It's also fine for someone who wants to start with a specific tea variety—Jasmine Green Tea—and doesn't want to hunt down a matching culture separately.
