### Frequently Asked Questions About the waatfeet 1700W Portable
### What kind of cookware works with this induction cooktop?
You need cookware with a magnetic bottom. The user manual includes a simple test: if a magnet sticks firmly to the bottom of the pot or pan, it is compatible. If the cooktop shows error code E0, it means no cookware was detected or the cookware isn't suitable.

### How do I set the timer, and what happens when it runs out?
You can set a timer up to 3 hours using the touch controls. Once the timer ends, the cooktop automatically shuts off, which helps with safety and energy savings.

### Can I adjust the heat level precisely?
Yes. The touch panel lets you select from 10 power levels, labeled P1 through P10. there's also a MAX button for maximum power when you need to boil or sear quickly.

### How do I clean the cooktop without damaging it?
Use a soft cloth and mild cleaning agents. Avoid abrasive scrubbers or strong acidic/alkaline cleaners to keep the panel's stain and scratch resistance intact.
