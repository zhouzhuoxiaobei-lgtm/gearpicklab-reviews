This mat claims to solve that with 1°F temperature precision and a timer, but the real question is whether its miniature heating surface and lack of wattage details actually deliver. The Prdingfy Sourdough Starter Warmer Mat is a dedicated heating pad for fermentation and proofing, aimed at home bakers who need stable temperatures for their starter jars. The specs show that offers an adjustable range from 70–100°F with 1°F increments, which is tighter control than many general-purpose heating mats that only offer low/medium/high settings. That kind of precision matters for sourdough—yeast activity changes noticeably with just a few degrees.
