 The drink range is the headline here. You get seven options: espresso, lungo, 8 oz (which is roughly a standard mug), double shot, latte, cappuccino, and macchiato. That covers the basics for most coffee drinkers — from a quick shot in the morning to a milk-based afternoon drink. The double shot function is a practical addition if you need more caffeine per cup without brewing twice. The built-in milk vessel means you don’t need a separate frother or steam wand. For someone who wants a latte or cappuccino without extra cleanup or equipment, this is the main convenience. The adjustable height for cups and milk preparation suggests you can fit different mug sizes under the spout, which is useful if you switch between espresso cups and taller travel mugs. Capsule compatibility is limited to Lavazza’s own Expert and Blue capsules. That’s a closed system — you can’t use Nespresso or other third-party pods. If you’re already buying Lavazza capsules, this is fine. If you want variety or cheaper alternatives, you’re locked into one brand’s lineup. Summary This machine suits someone who wants espresso and milk-based drinks from a single unit without a separate frother. The seven drink options cover most daily demands, and the built-in milk vessel simplifies cleanup. The capsule lock-in to Lavazza Expert and Blue capsules is the main trade-off — if you prefer a wider pod selection or want to use your own ground coffee, this isn’t the right fit. Based on the available specs, it’s a practical option for compact kitchens or offices where counter space is tight. Who this is for This makes sense if you’re already a Lavazza capsule user and want a machine that handles both black coffee and milk drinks. The built-in frother saves counter space and cleanup time — useful for a home office, petite apartment, or a break room where you don’t want multiple appliances. Looking at the data, adjustable height also helps if you use different cup sizes throughout the day. Who should look elsewhere If you want to use non-Lavazza capsules or prefer grinding your own beans, this machine won’t work. The closed capsule system limits your options.
