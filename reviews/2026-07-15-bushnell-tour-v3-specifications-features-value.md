### Frequently Asked Questions About the Bushnell Tour V3
### Does the Slope mode adjust yardage for uphill and downhill shots?
Yes, the Slope compensation feature calculates and adjusts swing yardage depending on the degree of the slope, so you get a corrected distance for uphill or downhill lies.

### How does the Pin Seeker with Jolt work?
Pin Seeker Technology locks onto the flag, and when it triggers, the device vibrates (Jolt) to confirm you've got the pin. SCAN mode gives a continuously updated LCD display as you pan across the landscape.

### What is the maximum range for flag measurement?
The rangefinder can measure distances from 10 to 1,000 yards overall, but specifically for a flag, it works up to 300 yards with +/- one yard of accuracy.

### Is the device easy to use with one hand?
Yes, it is designed for single-hand vertical operation with 5x magnification and a compact, ergonomic grip, making it easy to hold and operate with one hand.

### What comes in the box besides the rangefinder?
It includes a carrying case and a 3-volt battery, so you can use it right away. It also comes with a 2-year warranty.
