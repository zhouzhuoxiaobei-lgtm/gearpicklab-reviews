### Don't Make These Kitchen Appliances Buying Mistakes

### ❌ Mistake #1: Buying Based on Price Alone

Cheapest options often compromise on durability and performance.

**✅ Instead:** Focus on value — compare specs and read reviews.

### ❌ Mistake #2: Ignoring Specifications

Specs reveal what marketing hides. Ignoring them leads to poor choices.

**✅ Instead:** Read and compare spec sheets carefully.

### ❌ Mistake #3: Overlooking Compatibility

A great product is useless if it does not fit your setup.

**✅ Instead:** Check dimensions and compatibility before buying.

### ❌ Mistake #4: Skipping Research

Impulse buying leads to missed better options at the same price.

**✅ Instead:** Spend 15 minutes reading comparisons before purchasing.

### ❌ Mistake #5: Chasing Unnecessary Features

Paying for unused features wastes money.

**✅ Instead:** Focus on must-have features first.
